A Slimey Adventure

Instructions

W or ↑ - Move up A or ← - Move left
S or ↓ - Move down D or → - Move right

Have you ever felt that slimes have been under-represented as video-game protagonists? Well fear no more, because in this short Metroid-Vania inspired adventure game, you play as one of these living legends! The aim of the game is simple, get the crown and claim the title of King Slime you were always destined to earn.
